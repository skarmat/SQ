About Us

Welcome To Our Cottage

Star Quest Cottage provides a luxury experience on the Shore of Pangong Lake. The location of the cottage is covered with the breath-taking landscapes, from white deserts to blue lakes, snow-capped mountains to lush valleys, Ladakh, like a dream captured in a snow globe, is a wildly beautiful Mountain of this Trans Himalayas. Envisage yourself riding off into the sunset on a double hump camel with a Cottage turban draped around your head & the sun warmth on your face.
