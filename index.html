Frequently Asked Questions

Wake up to stunning vistas—snow-capped peaks, blue lakes, and endless valleys.

1. What amenities are included in the cottages?

Our luxury cottages offer heated rooms, en-suite bathrooms, premium bedding, and a private seating area with stunning views of Pangong Lake.

2. Is there Wi-Fi available at the property?

Yes, we provide complimentary Wi-Fi to keep you connected, though signal strength may vary due to the remote location.

3. What activities can guests enjoy during their stay?

Guests can enjoy stargazing, double-humped camel rides, bonfire nights, cultural experiences, and guided tours of nearby attractions.

4. Do you offer dining options on-site?

Yes, our in-house restaurant serves a variety of local Ladakhi delicacies and global cuisines, with special dining experiences by the lake.

5. Is Star Quest Cottage open year-round?

We operate seasonally from April to October due to extreme winter conditions.

5. Can special occasions or events be hosted at the property?

Absolutely! We offer customized packages for birthdays, anniversaries, and intimate celebrations with stunning backdrops.
